People Also Ask

1. How often should I water my plants?

The frequency of watering plants depends on various factors such as the type of plant, weather conditions, and the soil type. As a general rule of thumb, most plants require regular watering, ensuring the soil remains moist but not waterlogged.

2. What are some good organic fertilizers for my garden?

There are several organic fertilizers available, including compost, manure, seaweed extract, and bone meal. These natural fertilizers provide essential nutrients to your plants without the use of harmful chemicals.

3. How can I prevent weeds from overtaking my garden?

To prevent weeds, it's essential to use mulch, which acts as a protective barrier and prevents weed growth. Regularly removing any existing weeds by hand, without disturbing the soil, can also help keep your garden weed-free.
