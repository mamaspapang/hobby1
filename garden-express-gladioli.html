Q: How tall do Gladioli grow?
A: Gladioli can grow anywhere from 2 to 6 feet tall, depending on the variety.

Q: How often should I water Gladioli?
A: Gladioli should be watered regularly, about once a week, ensuring the soil remains moist but not waterlogged.

Q: Can Gladioli be grown from seeds?
A: No, Gladioli are typically propagated from corms, which are underground storage organs similar to bulbs.
