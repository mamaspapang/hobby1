Q: How often should I water my plants?

A: The watering frequency depends on several factors, such as the type of plant, weather conditions, and soil moisture. As a general rule, it's best to check the soil moisture regularly and water when it feels dry about an inch below the surface.

Q: How can I prevent pests and diseases in my garden?

A: There are several methods to prevent pests and diseases in your garden. These include maintaining proper hygiene, promoting good air circulation, using organic pest control methods, and regularly inspecting your plants for any signs of infestation or illness.

Q: Is it necessary to use fertilizers in gardening?

A: While not always necessary, fertilizers can provide essential nutrients to plants, promoting healthy growth and abundant blooms. However, it's important to use fertilizers sparingly and follow the instructions to prevent over-fertilization, which can harm the plants.
