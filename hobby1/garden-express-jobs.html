Q: How often should I water my garden?

A: The frequency of watering depends on various factors such as the type of plants, weather conditions, and soil moisture. Generally, it is advisable to water your garden deeply and infrequently rather than shallow and frequent watering.

Q: How can I prevent pests from damaging my plants?

A: There are several natural methods to keep pests at bay. You can try companion planting, using organic pest repellents, and maintaining a clean and healthy garden environment. Additionally, regular inspection and early intervention can help prevent pest infestations.
