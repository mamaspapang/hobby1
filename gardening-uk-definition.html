People also ask:

Can I garden without a backyard?

Absolutely! You can garden without a backyard by utilizing techniques like container gardening or vertical gardening. These methods allow you to grow plants in limited spaces such as balconies, patios, or even indoor areas near windows.

What are some low-maintenance plants for beginners?

For beginners, some easy-to-care-for plants include succulents, snake plants, pothos, and peace lilies. These plants are known for their ability to thrive with minimal care and are perfect for those new to gardening.

How do I prevent weeds in my garden?

To prevent weeds in your garden, you can apply mulch around your plants to suppress weed growth. Regularly pulling out weeds by their roots can also help keep them at bay. Additionally, planting densely and creating a well-maintained garden bed can minimize weed growth.
